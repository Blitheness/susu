My Very Very Super Extra Long News Story with Extra News

Swansea student Martin Hayes has beaten strong competition to become National Student Champion at the Student Kitesurfing Association Nationals over the weekend.

Martin entered the advance category and secured a win in a final that saw some very impressive tricks. Martin said "To become National Champion is an amazing feeling. I was not expecting to do so well".

Seven members of Swansea University kitesurfing club descended on Westward Ho! with a moderate 15---20 Mph winds. Swansea was amongst ten other universities and 115 competitors from across the country.

Alex Macadam, Mark Harris and Joel Edwards entered in the intermediate category with a very high standard of riding, a significant achievement for new riders Alex and Mark who have only taken the sport up since coming to university. Joel Edwards had a very good heat but narrowly missed out on the final due to a very high standard.

Frances Carey, the only girl to enter from the university, reached the final having spent a lot of her summer training in Fuerteventura.

The kitesurfing club would like to thank Kitemare and Flexifoil for their support. To find out more about kitesurfing go to https://www.facebook.com/groups/swanseauniversitykitesurfing/

Swansea University Students' Union, posted on 1st January 2013
