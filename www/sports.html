Sport Swansea

Sport Swansea is the home of Swansea University's student sports clubs.

Membership

To take part in sports at Swansea University you need to have a Sport Swansea Membership. This covers your insurance and helps to finance student sport.

Membership costs £10 for the whole academic year.
